An advanced secure framework for the education system

An advance secured framework for an educational system is designed to enhance the security of the class and online exam procedure for educational institutions such as schools, colleges, and private institutions to conduct course tests,taking classes for their students regularly. The system handles all operations from the beginning of IP based login system to block-chain based secure result publication that makes the education system more secure, user-friendly, time-saving, and effective. This system will try to resolve the critical obstruction of e-learning. The drawback of the online classes and exams can be overlooked with the help of Artificial Intelligent, Blockchain, RDP, and smart login systems. This proposed system will play a vital role in the evolution of e-learning, where teachers can take a proper class and exam just like they are physically present in the classroom.
